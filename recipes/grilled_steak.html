Grilled Steak


What is a grilled steak?

This is steak. Steak is a wonderful, meaty food that usually comes from a cow. There are many ways to cook a steak, but for this recipe, we will be grilling it.

Ingredients

  • 1 New York Strip Steak
  • Salt
  • Freshly Ground Black Pepper
  • Onion Powder
  • Garlic Powder

Tools Needed

  • Grill
  • Cutting Board
  • Meat Temperature Probe
  • Tongs
  • Plate

Steps

  1. Take your steak out of the package, and pat it dry completely with a paper towel.
  2. Lay your steak out on a cutting board.
  3. Season your steak to your liking with the salt, pepper, onion powder, and garlic powder.
  4. Stick the temperature probe in the steak.
  5. Light your grill and get it very hot.
  6. Once your grill is hot, lay your steak on the grill.
  7. Once happy with your sear, flip the steak using your tongs.
  8. Leave the steak on the uncharred side until you are happy with your sear.
  9. Once both sides are seared to your liking, lower your grill temperature and move your steak away from the flame to cook it indirectly until the internal temperature is 135F.
  10. Rest your steak on a plate for 3-5 minutes.
  11. After those 3-5 minutes, cut into your steak and enjoy!
