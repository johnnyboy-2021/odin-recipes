Scrambled Eggs


What are scrambled eggs?

These are scrambled eggs. Scrambled eggs are a breakfast food made out of eggs.

Ingredients

  • Egg
  • Salt
  • Freshly Ground Black Pepper
  • Onion Powder
  • Garlic Powder

Tools Needed

  • Pan
  • Stove
  • Spatula
  • Plate

Steps

  1. Light your stove and put the pan over the burner.
  2. Crack your egg into the pan.
  3. Mix up the egg with your spatula.
  4. Season your egg with salt, pepper, onion powder, and garlic powder.
  5. As soon as there is no more liquid from the egg, remove it and place it on the plate.
  6. Enjoy!
